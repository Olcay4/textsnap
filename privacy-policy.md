# Privacy Policy for TextSnap

Last updated: March 28, 2026

TextSnap does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Data Processing

All OCR processing is performed entirely on your device using a locally bundled copy of Tesseract.js. No screenshot, image, or extracted text is ever sent to any server or third party.

## Local Storage

Extracted text history is stored locally in your browser using `chrome.storage.local`. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time using the "Clear History" button in the extension.

## No Tracking

The extension does not use analytics, tracking cookies, or any external services.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/Olcay4/textsnap).
